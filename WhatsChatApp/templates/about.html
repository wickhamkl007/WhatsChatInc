About page

WhatsChat is a basic chat app that was created by Taylor Powell, Blaze Wiseman, and Kavin Wickham for a university project. The students attend the University of Montana in Missoula, MT and the class it was created for is CSCI 291 under the instruciton of Patricia Duce. This app is intended to be basic and have a simple funciton of allowing two users to chat back and forth.
